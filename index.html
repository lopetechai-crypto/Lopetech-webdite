FAQ.

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's everything you need to know about working with us on custom AI workflows, agents, databases, and high-performance software.

What kinds of custom AI agents and LLM integrations do you build?

We build autonomous AI agents tailored to automate complex operations, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems that connect LLMs to your private data secure databases, and custom model fine-tuning utilizing state-of-the-art frameworks like LangChain, OpenAI, and Claude.

How do you ensure data security and privacy when developing AI solutions?

Security is our core priority. We construct sandboxed vector databases, leverage enterprise-grade APIs with zero-data retention policies, and implement local, self-hosted LLMs when necessary to guarantee your proprietary business intelligence remains completely confidential and private.

What tech stacks do you leverage for high-performance apps and websites?

We build lightning-fast web experiences using modern architectures like Next.js, React, Node.js, and TailwindCSS. For mobile apps, we deploy cross-platform architectures using Flutter or React Native, alongside native Kotlin and Swift integrations to achieve maximum performance and native UI response.

Can you integrate custom AI workflows into our existing legacy systems?

Absolutely. We engineer custom API layers, webhooks, and middleware that act as a bridge between your legacy databases or enterprise systems and modern AI pipelines, allowing you to scale intelligent automation without rewriting your core infrastructure.

Who owns the final code, custom AI models, and intellectual property?

You do. Once project milestones are complete and finalized, 100% of the custom codebase, database schemas, custom model parameters, and intellectual property are fully transferred to your business with zero recurring licensing fees.
